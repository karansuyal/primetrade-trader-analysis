# Trader Performance vs Market Sentiment Analysis

## Methodology
- Merged Fear/Greed sentiment data with Hyperliquid trader data
- Analyzed 479 days of overlapping data (2024-2025)
- Compared performance metrics across Fear vs Greed days

## Key Findings

### 1. Performance Difference
- **Fear days**: ₹39,012 average daily PnL
- **Greed days**: ₹15,848 average daily PnL
- **Fear days outperform by 146%**

### 2. Trading Behavior
- **Fear days**: 793 trades/day (aggressive)
- **Greed days**: 294 trades/day (cautious)
- **Activity increases 2.7x during Fear**

### 3. Win Rate
- **Greed days**: 38.5% win rate
- **Fear days**: 32.9% win rate
- Greed days show better accuracy but lower profits

## Strategy Recommendations

1. **Contrarian Position Sizing**
   - Increase position size by 30-40% during Fear days
   
2. **Frequency Optimization**
   - Maintain high trading activity during Fear days
   
3. **Dynamic Risk Management**
   - Tighter stop-losses during Fear days
   - Normal parameters during Greed days
